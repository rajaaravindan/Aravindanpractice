Tiruchirappalli[b] (Tamil pronunciation: [ˈt̪iɾɯtːʃiɾaːpːaɭːi] ⓘ, formerly Trichinopoly in English), also called Tiruchi or Trichy, is a major tier II city in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu and the administrative headquarters of Tiruchirappalli district. The city is credited with being the best livable city,[10] the cleanest city of Tamil Nadu, as well as the fifth safest city for women in India.[11] It is the fourth largest city as well as the fourth largest urban agglomeration in the state.
